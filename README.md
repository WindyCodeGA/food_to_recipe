**# 🍲 Food to Recipe | Your Smart Kitchen Assistant

Turn everyday ingredients into culinary masterpieces with the power of Generative AI.

## 📖 Overview

**Food to Recipe** is your ultimate digital sous-chef. Designed to eliminate the daily "what's for dinner" dilemma, this application analyzes your available ingredients—whether manually entered or scanned from a receipt—and instantly generates tailored recipes. By leveraging advanced Generative AI, it adapts to your dietary needs and preferences, delivering a seamless, cross-platform experience built entirely on Flutter.

## 🚀 Core Features

* 🧠 **AI-Driven Recipe Engine:** Harness the power of Generative AI to instantly create unique, step-by-step cooking instructions from scratch.
* 🧾 **Smart Ingredient Scanner:** Quickly extract grocery items from your shopping receipts using high-precision OCR technology.
* 💎 **Elegant User Experience:** A sleek, modern interface carefully crafted for ease of use right in the kitchen.
* ⚡ **Seamless Cloud Sync:** Secure user authentication and real-time database management backed by Firebase.
* 🌐 **True Cross-Platform:** Smooth, native-like performance on both iOS and Android devices.
* 📥 **Pocket Recipe Book:** Save and organize your favorite AI-generated meals locally for offline access anytime.
